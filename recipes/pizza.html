Easy Tortilla Pizza

Description

Looking for something to eat that is both yummy and little to no cook/prep time.
Try making the a tortilla pizza. It is a quick and easy meal that can be made in under 20 minutes.
Try it out, because who does not love pizza?

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Oil
  • Seasoning
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Toppings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Brush the tortilla with olive and seasoning.
  2. Bake the tortilla
  3. Remove the tortilla
  4. Top the tortilla with tomato sauce
  5. Add toppings
  6. Sprinkle it with cheese
  7. Bake again until the cheese melts, and season with oregano
